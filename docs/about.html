About

What The 4th Pig Built

From the ashes of the Port O-Call rises The Green Pig Pub in all it's glory. We strive to be an eco-friendly pub that is truely Green and has more to offer than any other. We've got a delicious food menu that has no comparison and accomodates to all variety of dishes including vegan and gluten free. But that's not all we have to offer. With live music 4 nights a week and group trivia events that get everyone using their noggins. You can't find any better pub, especially with the amazing staffing crew that really makes you feel like a V.I.P. every night you come to hang out. It's a great place to meet up with friends or make new ones.
