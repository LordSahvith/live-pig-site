The Pig

This is The Green Pig Pub of downtown Salt Lake City. Home to a everything pub. Drinks, food, sports and live music. We support our local sports teams and Universities by showing their games up on any of our 15 flat screen tvs. And if sports aren't your thing then don't fret because there's an event going on everyday. We've got trivia Wednesdays and Sundays nights, where you can test your wit against other groups to be the biggest know-it-all or check out Monday nights for Blues Jam, Friday for live bands and Saturday for DJ Latu. Don't worry we didn't forget about you shameless singers and we gave you a karaoke night on Thursdays.
