Daily Events

Open Blues Jam

If you love playing, listening and dancing to blues then Monday nights at The Green Pig is the place for you. We have a live local blues band "West Temple Tail Draggers" that open up the night with a sign up sheet for others to join so bring your instrument and sign up to show off your passion for blues.

Locals Night out

Have an itch for painting but aren't familiar with the best techniques and practices? Too shy to paint in front of others? Grab a friend, a cup of liquid courage and come test your painting skills!

General Trivia

Think your a trivia wizard? Grab some witty friends and come test your knowledge against other teams for a chance to win a Green Pig Gift Certificate or just plain ol' bragging rights! Call to reserve your team's spot now. Oh by the way...Daisy can be quite ferocious so if this isn't the first question you'd better put your phone away...

Karaoke

Got the courage to sing your heart out before an audience? Come kick it with Nirvana (your hostess) and show everybody your impressive singing voice or give a shameless performance for all to enjoy! Singing starts @ 9 so come early to get your spot.

Live Music

If you're looking for some good Friday night fun, look no further. Every Friday at 10pm we have live music from a range of genres. So grab your friends and come have a night to remember.

DJ Latu

Say What?! DJ Latu from U92? That's right. Come grab some drinks with your friends and dance the night away, while kickin with local sensation Latu!

Breakfast Buffet / Adult Trivia

Our Breakfast Buffet starts at 10am and goes to 2pm so come grab some grub with a delicious Mimosa or Bloody Mary. We have omelets cooked to order and a variety of breakfast items including Vegan.
